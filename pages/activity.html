Nature Needs Us

Empowering teens across Europe to learn and act for the environment.

About the Project

“Nature Needs Us” is a project developed in collaboration with our partners and students from 4 schools from Romania, North Macedonia, Hungary and Czech Republic, as a solution to provide teenagers of 13-17 years old a context to learn about environmental protection and eco-friendly practices through non-formal activities, as their educational institutions do not provide such subjects for them.

Why It Matters

It is important, especially for teenagers, to learn about sustainable and eco-friendly practices and the need of protecting the environment, particularly in this period of time, when we see more accentuated climate changes around us. Learning such practices from a young age can shape an individual more anchored into the environmental reality of the moment, understanding the importance of taking action and being environmental-responsible, empowering the ones around them in doing so as well.

Project Goal

The goal of our project is to increase the green-skills and environmental knowledge of 32 teenagers between 13-17 years old from Romania, Czech Republic, Hungary and North Macedonia, through non-formal activities, by the end of the project.
