About UBDB

Learn more about our mission, values, and the impact we're making

Our Story

UBDB is a non-governmental and non-profit organization founded in 2024 with a clear vision: to bring young people together at a European and global scale, regardless of nationality, religion, or sexual orientation. We believe in the power of connection, understanding, and shared experiences to create a better world.

Our Mission

Our main purpose is to create opportunities for young people to get to know each other and have fun on a journey full of social, cultural, and sports activities. We aim to form figures that demonstrate correct and suitable behaviors for the 21st century through our diverse range of programs.

Our Values

🤝

We believe in the power of working together to achieve common goals and create positive change.

💡

❤️

We foster understanding and compassion among young people from different cultures and backgrounds.

What We Do

Through our activities, we focus on several key areas:

Intercultural Activities

Our participants learn about different cultures and habits, breaking down barriers and building bridges of understanding. These experiences help young people develop a global perspective and appreciate the richness of diversity.

Formal and Non-Formal Learning

We believe that learning through fun activities is more efficient because it's less stressful and more natural. Our programs combine structured learning with interactive experiences that engage participants and make knowledge stick.

Philanthropy Activities

We aim to help and improve the quality of young people's lives by giving them opportunities to travel, meet new people, and create strong relationships. These experiences can be life-changing and open doors to new possibilities.

Our Impact

Since our founding, UBDB has been dedicated to encouraging young people to follow their dreams, realize their potential, and live healthier, more fulfilling lives. We create an inclusive community where everyone belongs and can thrive.

Our organization supports embracing the differences between people, whether they relate to religion, sexual orientation, or nationality. Together, we create a future where youth can thrive—not just survive.
